Root Cause Analysis

Primary Issue: Agents are under-charging — 0.058 sessions/agent/day vs real-world 1.1.

Reason: ChangeChargingBehaviour strategy adds " charging" to activities at ~25%/iteration rate. By iteration 5, only ~33K of 130K agents have home charging in their plans. The remaining 75% drive without ever plugging in.

What validates well: Pricing (exact MD rates), stuck agents (<0.1%), work kWh/session, car trips/day.

Expected by iteration 30: Home charging coverage ~70-80%, sessions 3-5x higher, home energy share ~60-70%.

For full convergence: May need 50-60 iterations or faster exploration rate.

Benchmark Sources

  • INL EV Project (2015) — 8,300 vehicles tracked with EVSE data loggers. Gold standard for US charging behavior.
  • NREL (2021-2023) — ChargePoint residential data analysis. National charging patterns.
  • EPRI / Edison Electric Institute (2018-2022) — Energy share by charging location.
  • US DOE AFDC (2023-2024) — Alternative Fuel Station data and pricing.
  • EPA / FHWA (2022-2024) — Energy consumption rates and VMT data.
  • JD Power EV Experience Study (2023) — Consumer charging behavior survey.
  • UC Davis PH&EV Center (2019-2021) — California EV owner surveys.
